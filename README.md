# Chocolate Sales Analysis — Power BI

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which team generates highest sales and profit?
2. Which salesperson has highest variance from target?
3. Are we meeting monthly targets?
4. Who performs best per month?
5. Which product has highest profit per box?
6. What does the sales forecast look like?

## Key Insights
- Yummies team leads in both sales and profit
- Overall business is -1.15% below target
- Milk Bars has highest profit per box at $23.1
- Sales hike during August 2022
- Variance per sale person is highest in Octber month
- Data trend is going down-hill in next few months
